Events

Bench Yell

Bench yell events, often called cheer rallies, are energetic gatherings where students and teams unite to perform chants and cheers. These activities build camaraderie, inspire enthusiasm, and spark competitive energy.

Tekno Modelo

Tekno Modelo is a creative competition where participants design and present outfits inspired by gods and goddesses, blending mythology with artistic innovation. It celebrates creativity, cultural heritage, and imaginative storytelling through fashion.

Pasiklaban

Pasiklaban is a vibrant competition that highlights acting performances, where participants showcase their talent, creativity, and passion for storytelling on stage. It celebrates dramatic arts and the spirit of friendly rivalry.

Debate

Debate is an intellectual competition where participants engage in structured arguments, presenting and defending their perspectives on various topics. It fosters critical thinking, public speaking, and respectful discourse.

Esports

Esports, short for electronic sports, is a competitive gaming phenomenon where players and teams face off in video game tournaments. These events combine skill, strategy, and teamwork, attracting global audiences and fostering a vibrant gaming community.

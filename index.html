Bench Yell

Bench yell events, or cheer rallies, are lively gatherings where students unite for chants and cheers, fostering camaraderie, enthusiasm, and team spirit.

Tekno Modelo

Tekno Modelo is a creative competition where participants design and present outfits inspired by gods and goddesses, blending mythology with artistic innovation. It celebrates creativity, cultural heritage, and imaginative storytelling through fashion.

Pasiklaban

Pasiklaban is a vibrant competition that highlights acting performances, where participants showcase their talent, creativity, and passion for storytelling on stage. It celebrates dramatic arts and the spirit of friendly rivalry.

Debate

Debate is an intellectual competition where participants engage in structured arguments, presenting and defending their perspectives on various topics. It fosters critical thinking, public speaking, and respectful discourse.

Esports

Esports, or electronic sports, are competitive gaming tournaments where players showcase skill, strategy, and teamwork, uniting a global gaming community.

Hackathon

A hackathon, also known as a codefest, is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon. They have become hotbeds of technology, collaboration, and learning.

Palarong Pinoy

Palarong Pinoy is a traditional games competition that showcases classic Filipino games, fostering camaraderie, teamwork, and a celebration of Filipino culture.

Traditional Arts

Traditional Arts is a celebration of heritage and craftsmanship, where participants showcase skills in creating culturally inspired artworks. It honors history, creativity, and the richness of traditional Filipino artistry.

Digital Arts

Digital Arts is a creative competition where participants use technology to craft visually stunning and innovative digital artworks. It highlights modern creativity, technical skills, and artistic expression in the digital age.

Animation

Animation is a dynamic competition where participants bring stories to life through moving visuals, showcasing creativity, storytelling, and technical artistry. It celebrates innovation and the magic of animated storytelling.
